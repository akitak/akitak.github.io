Sea Level Rise near Alaska

This project was a collaboration with 3 other data scientists to assess climate change, specifically in Alaska, and the effects on sea level. This project demonstrates a variety of competencies, beginning from analyzing scientific data from government and research agencies, to performing predictions using a variety of machine learning and deep learning models. A variety of metrics were used to assess the performance of our model, with an ultimate correlation coefficient of 96.39%, with an RMSE of 192.95 using a Random Forest model.

Natural Language Processing - Classification of Reddit Posts

This project uses natural language processing techniques to assess and successfully classify Reddit posts from the two official subreddits of the Premier League teams Manchester City and Manchester United. My model ultimately predicts which subreddit any given post comes from with an accuracy of 88.67% when making predictions using a test set.

Deforestation of the Pacific Temperate Rainforests

This project is a work in-progress, with the ultimate goal of exploring the deforestation of temperate rainforests. I begin this project by refining my research to the Pacific Temperate Rainforest, specifically in the Central Coast region of British Columbia, which contains a portion of the Great Bear Rainforest.
